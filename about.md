# About the Artist  
### Gerald Allen Knowles — Architect • Artist • Creative Director

GAK Creations is the lifelong artistic journey of **Gerald Allen Knowles**, an architect and visual storyteller shaped by more than four decades of creative exploration across Europe, the Caribbean, and the Canary Islands.

Born with an early fascination for how things are built — in nature, in cities, in the quiet geometry of everyday life — Gerald’s work blends the precision of architectural training with the emotional depth of lived experience. His art is rooted in observation: staircases in Saintes, coastlines in Fuerteventura, the warm light of the Bahamas, the textured histories of France and Spain.

---

## ✦ A Life in Lines, Light, and Movement

Gerald’s career spans **45+ years** of architecture, teaching, and experiential learning. His time at the *Centre d’Étude d’Architecture et d’Urbanisme* in Saintes, France, shaped his understanding of form, rhythm, and spatial storytelling. Travel deepened it — each place adding a new layer of color, memory, and meaning.

Today, based in **Fuerteventura**, he continues to draw from the landscapes around him: volcanic horizons, Atlantic winds, and the quiet poetry of island life.

---

## ✦ The Vision Behind GAK Creations

GAK Creations is more than a brand — it is a living archive of sketches, prints, and visual narratives. Each piece carries:

- the discipline of architectural drawing  
- the spontaneity of travel  
- the emotion of memory  
- the clarity of minimalist design  

Gerald’s work invites viewers to slow down, observe, and rediscover the beauty in structure, shadow, and place.
