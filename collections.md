# Art Collections  
### A curated archive of artwork by Gerald Allen Knowles

Explore the evolving world of **GAK Creations** — a blend of architecture, travel, coastal landscapes, and global inspiration. Each collection reflects a different chapter of a life lived across continents.

---

## ✦ Architectural Sketches
Hand‑drawn studies of staircases, cloisters, façades, and historic structures.  
A dialogue between precision and emotion.

Prints inspired by the Atlantic, the Bahamas, and the volcanic geometry of Fuerteventura.  
Color, rhythm, and shoreline memory.  
[Explore coastal pieces](https://www.gakcreations.com)
